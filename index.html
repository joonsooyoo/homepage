I am a PhD student at Korea University, center for information security.

Bio

I received my BS in Mathematics from Korea University (KU) in 2017 and currently pursuing MS degree in Information Security at KU which is expected to be finished in February 2020. I am currently working with Ji Won Yoon in Signal Processing and Advanced Intelligence (SPAI) Lab as a project manager of 'Research on the Design and Utilization of Machine Learning Library on Encrypted Data' supported by Institute of Information & Communications Technology Planning & Evaluation.

Current Research Interest

My current research is centered around the design and implementation of Machine Learning algorithms on the encrypted domain using Homomorphic Encryption (HE) scheme. In recent days, I am particularly interested in constructing HE sampling algorithm through the concept of Markov Chain Monte Carlo.

Future Research Interest

I am currently looking for PhD program in Machine Learning. I prefer researching the fundamental concepts and algorithms in ML without losing a sense of its realistic applications. I hope that its application lies in biology where I had relevant experience.
